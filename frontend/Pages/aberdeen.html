Discover More About Aberdeen's Landmarks

Delve deeper into the unique features of Aberdeen’s most iconic sites.

Marischal College

An architectural marvel, Marischal College is a testament to Aberdeen’s granite heritage. This striking building is now home to Aberdeen City Council and serves as a cultural landmark.

Union Terrace Gardens

A tranquil oasis in the bustling city, Union Terrace Gardens offers a serene spot to relax and enjoy nature.

Aberdeen Beach

Known for its golden sands and beautiful promenade, Aberdeen Beach is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.

St Machar's Cathedral

A gem of medieval architecture, St Machar's Cathedral is a must-visit landmark that tells the story of Aberdeen’s religious past.
